Chocolate Chip Cookies

By: Dan the Baker Man

  • 2 1/4 cup of flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cup of chocolate chips
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl.
  3. Cream together butter and sugar until smooth.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time.
  5. Gradually add flour mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 9–11 minutes.
